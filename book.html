About the author

SM

Stephen Mallett is a former Headteacher and SENCo with twenty-five years inside the UK education system. He has led schools through inspection, overseen complex SEND provision, written and defended EHCPs, and made the calls to Local Authorities that parents are usually the wrong side of.

He is also a parent. The framework in this book is the one he reached for when his own family needed it — and the one he could not find on a shelf.

He is the founder of SEND Navigate, which provides specialist EHCP and tribunal advisory support to families and the See It. Support It. Teach It. SEND training programme to schools and MATs.

He is the author of Parent Shield, the first volume in The SEND Navigate Library.

If you want your child's school to actually understand this material
