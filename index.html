Built by someone who sat in both chairs.

Stephen Mallett spent 25 years working in and leading UK schools — as a classroom teacher, SENCo, and Headteacher. He is the Founder of SEND Navigate and the author of Parent Shield and When Your Child Can't Go to School.

As a parent of two children with SEN, Stephen brings a dual perspective that is rare in this field: the insider knowledge of a school leader and the lived experience of a parent navigating the same system he once ran. That combination is the foundation of every SEND Navigate training programme and advisory case.

His free parent guides are used by thousands of families across the UK. His school training programme is designed to close the gap between what EHCPs say and what actually happens in classrooms.
