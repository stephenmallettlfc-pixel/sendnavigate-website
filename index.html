For Parents & Carers

The SEND system is designed to be confusing. We know every move they make.

Local Authorities delay assessments, issue inadequate EHCPs, and refuse requests — banking on the fact that most parents do not know their rights well enough to push back effectively. Schools are under-resourced and under pressure. And you are left exhausted, confused, and fighting alone for a child who deserves better.

SEND Navigate was built to change that. Stephen Mallett spent 25 years inside the system — as a teacher, a SENCo, and a Headteacher. He has two children of his own with SEN. He knows exactly what the law requires, what the LA is obligated to do, and where they fall short. When you come to SEND Navigate, you are not alone any more.

About Stephen Mallett

25 years inside the SEND system. Two children with SEN of his own.

Stephen Mallett spent 25 years working in UK schools — as a classroom teacher, a SENCo, and a Headteacher. He has seen the SEND system from every angle: the school that is under-resourced, the LA that delays and deflects, and the parent who is exhausted and alone.

He also knows that experience from the other side. Stephen has two children of his own with SEN. He has sat in the meetings, read the letters, and felt the frustration of a system that is supposed to help but often does not. That dual perspective — professional expertise and personal experience — is what makes SEND Navigate different.

Stephen is also the author of two books on SEN rights for parents, published on Amazon, and the creator of a CPD-accredited SEN training programme for schools and MATs.
